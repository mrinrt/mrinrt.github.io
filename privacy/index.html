Privacy Policy

Privacy Statement

 

This privacy policy will explain how mrinrt.org uses personal data we collect from you when you use our website.

 

How do we collect your data?

You directly provide mrinrt.org with the data we collect. We collect data and process data only when you complete the questionnaire regarding your intention to participate in the MRinRT Symposium 2025.

 

How will we use your data?

mrinrt.org collects your data so that we can collect applications for the participation in the planned event. You data will not be shared with third parties.

 

How do we store your data?

mrinrt.org securely collects your data via Google Forms that are embedded into our website. The submitted information will be stored in a secure and protected way in Google Forms and in Google Sheets.

 

What are your data protection rights?

mrinrt.org would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

The right to access – You have the right to request mrinrt.org for copies of your personal data.

The right to rectification – You have the right to request that mrinrt.org correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request mrinrt.org to complete the information you believe is incomplete.

The right to erasure – You have the right to request that mrinrt.org erase your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that mrinrt.org restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to mrinrt.org’s processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to data portability – You have the right to request that mrinrt.org transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our email: mrinrt2025@gmail.com

 

Cookies

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our websites, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology.

 

How do we use cookies?

mrinrt.org currently does not use cookies.

 

Privacy policies of other websites

The mrinrt.org website may contain links to other websites. Our privacy policy applies only to our website, so if you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.

 

Changes to our privacy policy

mrinrt.org keeps its privacy policy under regular review and places any updates on this web page. This privacy policy was last updated on 21 June 2026.

 

How to contact us

If you have any questions about MRinRT Symposium 2025 privacy policy, the data we hold on you, or you would like to exercise one of your data protection rights, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Email us at: mrinrt2025@gmail.com
