Abstract Submission

Abstract submission for MRinRT 2026 will open at end of November 2025.
The deadline for submission is January 11, 2026, 23:59 CET.

Abstract Topics

Clinical outcomes

Data from MRI based clinical trials and MRI-linac trials

Quantitative MRI and biomarkers

MRI biomarkers, functional imaging, treatment adaptation

AI and workflow efficiency

Contouring, adaptation, automation, decision support, MR-only workflows

Technical and physics innovations

Sequence optimization, image quality, low-field MRI

Image quality and QA

MR dosimetry, QA tool, MRI quality

Clinical trials and collaborative data initiatives

Trial proposals, Trial design, multi-center integration, data harmonization

Best practice multidisciplinary collaborations

We invite institutions to tell their story about how they interact with radiology departments (radiologist, MR physicist, radiographers) in their clinical workflow. There will be a specific session for this topic, with representation of each role/background.

Young Investigator Awards

It will be possible to submit an abstract as young investigator for nomination to the young investigator award
