For Authors

Abstract Format

The abstract is limited to 500 words and may include one table and up to two figures. It should be structured into the following sections: Purpose, Methods, Materials, Results and Conclusions. A two-page supporting document is optional but encouraged. Neither the abstract nor the disclosure should contain identifying information such as title, author names, affiliations, institutions, etc. Submissions containing such information may be rejected without review. Abstracts should be original work that has not been previously presented or published in a journal (work submitted but not accepted for publication is acceptable).

Submission Categories

  • Clinical studies and application
  • MR for treatment planning
  • In-room MRI for radiotherapy guidance
  • QA/QC, calibration and MR Safety
  • MR based motion assessment and management
  • New MR sequences and novel techniques/applications
  • Quantitative imaging and treatment response assessment
  • Advanced and special topics (AI, data science, dose accumulation etc)
  • Young investigator award competition
